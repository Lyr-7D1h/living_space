Living Space

Welcome to Living Space! This is a simulation of a small ecosystem where creatures interact with each other and their environment. You can create new creatures by clicking on the canvas. Each creature has a unique personality that influences how they interact with their environment and other creatures. You can also change the environment by clicking and dragging the background. Have fun!
